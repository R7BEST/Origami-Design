INTERESTING FACT ABOUT CAMEL

1)Camels are known as the "Ship Of The Desert"

2)Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.

3)Their humps let them store up to 80 pounds of fat which they can live off for weeks and even months!

INTERESTING FACT ABOUT CHAMELEON

1)Chameleon changes it's body color as per their background.

2)Refering to the last fact Chameleon changes it's body color for either regulating the body temperature or for protecing it's self from it's predators.

3)Most Chameleon have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

INTERESTING FACT ABOUT PIGEON

1)Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.

2)Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two chicks at the same time.

3)Both female and male pigeons share responsibility of caring for and raising young.
